ZAFPI Production Reporting Digitization
Zamboanga Agri-Farm Products Inc. produces eggs for wholesale buyers in Zamboanga City, Jolo Island, and Tawi-Tawi.
Two-site production reporting is manual and paper-based, with T+1 visibility and weekly reconciliation needed for about 43% of documents.
Move to same-day visibility, stronger data integrity, faster operational review, and tighter management control.
