Flow

Current state to target state.

1. Record on paper
Production data starts as manual site paperwork across two locations.
2. Delay to T+1
Management waits until the next reporting cycle for consolidated visibility.
3. Reconcile
About 43% of documents need weekly reconciliation before confidence improves.
4. Encode same-day
The target workflow turns each pass into a record earlier in the operating day.
5. Review within the day
Management reviews dashboards, daily reports, and KPI signals within the same day.
