ZAFPI snapshot

Why it matters

The project is about control, not just software.

Before Farmbrite, ZAFPI relied on a manual, paper-based production reporting process. That slowed management review, weakened feed and mortality visibility per house, and forced weekly reconciliation on a large share of records.

43% of documents require reconciliation, utilization sits around 57%, and management still waits for delayed visibility. Project Farmbrite exists to change those conditions into a disciplined, same-day operating rhythm.
