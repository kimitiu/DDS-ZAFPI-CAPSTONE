Results and value

The project aims to create cleaner discipline and faster management response.

Project Farmbrite defines success through same-day visibility, lower reconciliation, stronger reporting discipline, and improved management control. The expected value is not only better records, but a more reliable operating rhythm for planning, review, and daily action.

If the system stabilizes as intended, ZAFPI should be better positioned to track feed and mortality more consistently, improve reporting confidence, and move toward stronger utilization performance.

Results framing

The capstone measures success by the quality of the operating model after rollout, not by system access alone.

Visibility Same-day
Operational review should be available on the same day instead of arriving in a T+1 cycle.
Reconciliation <15%
The target is to reduce the current 43% reconciliation burden after stabilization.
Encoding discipline 95%+
Daily reporting compliance is one of the clearest indicators that the operating model is working.
Utilization pathway ~75%
A better-controlled, more visible operation supports the path from 57% baseline utilization toward improved performance.

Before Project Farmbrite

The current-state process limits confidence and slows response.

  • T+1 reporting delay
  • 43% weekly reconciliation burden
  • Weak house-level feed visibility
  • No formal consolidated monthly, quarterly, or yearly reports

After stabilization

The target state is a more reliable operating model for daily management review.

  • Same-day visibility for operations
  • Lower reconciliation and cleaner reporting discipline
  • Improved feed and mortality tracking
  • Stronger readiness for planning and operational review

Value summary

Stronger reporting discipline

Daily data becomes more complete, timely, and consistent enough to support regular management review.

Improved management control

Earlier visibility gives decision-makers more room to respond instead of only documenting issues after the fact.

Better planning readiness

Cleaner reporting supports future trend review, operational analysis, and higher-confidence planning.

Expected capability gains

Same-day visibility

Operational signals become visible within the day rather than after the reporting cycle has passed.

Feed and mortality tracking

Key house-level measures can be reviewed more consistently with structured source capture and reporting.

Utilization improvement pathway

Better control and cleaner visibility create the conditions for more confident utilization improvement over time.
