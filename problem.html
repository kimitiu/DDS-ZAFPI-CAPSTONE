Problem

Manual reporting slows operational control.

Paper-based reporting across two sites

ZAFPI’s production reporting process depends on manual documents and delayed consolidation, which limits same-day visibility for management.

T+1 visibility

Key production information is not immediately available for daily review, making corrective action slower and less precise.

43% weekly reconciliation burden

Around 43% of documents require weekly reconciliation, signaling data inconsistency, additional rework, and lower confidence in routine reporting.

57% utilization baseline

The current state points to substantial room for better monitoring, tighter execution, and stronger use of available production capacity.

The capstone problem statement is not only about digitizing forms. It is about improving data integrity, operational review, and management control so decisions can happen on the same day rather than after the fact.
