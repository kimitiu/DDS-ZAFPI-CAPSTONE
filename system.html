Farmbrite Demo
System

From field encoding to management review.

The solution strategy combines business process redesign with an off-the-shelf system approach using Farmbrite. The goal is to make data capture structured, validation immediate, reporting visible, and control enforceable through a near real-time operating model.

1. Data capture layerField encoding through structured forms and validation rules.
2. Data processing layerAutomated checks, reconciliation logic, and aggregation.
3. Reporting layerDashboards, daily reports, and KPI tracking.
4. Control layerSOPs, approval flow, audit trail, and accountability.
Workflow Diagram
Core Workflows
Daily production flowEgg production occurs at each house, data is captured immediately, validated, synced to a central dashboard, and reviewed within the same day.
Feed tracking flowFeed issuance per house is recorded, consumption is compared with output, and variances are flagged automatically.
Mortality tracking flowMortality is recorded by house, time-stamped, linked to flock data, and included in the daily reporting view.
Data governance flowCompleteness checks, correction notes, audit trails, and data locking support accountability after validation.
