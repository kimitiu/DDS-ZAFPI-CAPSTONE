System

From field encoding to management review.

The proposed system direction is a same-day reporting workflow that turns production events into structured records earlier in the day, improving visibility, data integrity, and oversight during rollout and go-live.

1. CaptureDaily production information is encoded closer to the source instead of staying on paper until later consolidation.
2. ValidateStandardized entry reduces document mismatches and lowers the weekly reconciliation load.
3. ConsolidateOperational data becomes available for same-day review across the farm rather than waiting for T+1 reporting.
4. ReviewManagement gains stronger control over utilization, compliance, house activation progress, and follow-up actions.

The homepage simulation translates this flow into a visual sequence: the egg pass represents a production event, the overlay acts as an operations dashboard, and the tablet reflects the intended shift from lagging paperwork to near-real-time review.
