Farmbrite Demo
System

From field encoding to management review.

The solution combines business process redesign with an off-the-shelf system strategy using Farmbrite. The point is to make data capture structured, validation immediate, reporting visible, and control enforceable through a same-day operating model.

1. Data capture layerField encoding through structured forms and validation rules.
2. Data processing layerAutomated checks, reconciliation logic, and aggregation.
3. Reporting layerDashboards, daily reports, and KPI tracking.
4. Control layerSOPs, approval flow, audit trails, and accountability.
Workflow Diagram
Paper sourceCurrent-state field records begin on paper across the operation.
EncodingSame-day entry converts paperwork into structured operational records.
ValidationIntegrity checks, reconciliation logic, and exception handling improve trust in the data.
ReviewManagement uses the resulting dashboard and daily reports for operational control.
Core Workflows
Daily production flowEgg production occurs at each house, data is captured immediately, validated, synced to a central dashboard, and reviewed within the same day.
Feed tracking flowFeed issued per house is recorded, consumption is tracked against output, and variances are flagged automatically.
Mortality tracking flowMortality is recorded per house, time-stamped, linked to flock data, and included in daily reports.
Data governance flowCompleteness checks, correction notes, audit trails, and locking rules support accountability after validation.
