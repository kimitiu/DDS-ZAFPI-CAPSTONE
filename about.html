About

ZAFPI’s reporting modernization initiative.

Project Farmbrite is a data-driven operational transformation initiative for Zamboanga Agri-Farm Products Inc. It redesigns the company’s production reporting and monitoring approach, moving from fragmented, manual, paper-based workflows into a structured digital system centered on same-day visibility, cleaner data capture, and stronger management control.

Egg productionZAFPI operates as an agricultural enterprise focused on egg production and wholesale distribution.
3 marketsZamboanga City, Jolo Island, and Tawi-Tawi form the core service area.
24 houses19 Layer Houses, 2 Brooder Houses, and 3 Grower Houses.
57% baselineCurrent utilization level that frames the improvement opportunity.
Objectives
Operational objective
Digitize production reporting, standardize workflows, centralize data, and reduce manual error.
Data objective
Capture structured daily data on egg output, rejects, mortality, flock age and headcount, and feed issuance and consumption.
System objective
Use dashboards, validation rules, audit trails, role-based access, and automated reports to support control.
Positioning

Project Farmbrite is positioned as an operational management system, not just a software installation. The website uses the simulation to make that shift understandable at a glance.
