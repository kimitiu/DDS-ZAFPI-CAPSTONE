About

ZAFPI’s reporting modernization initiative.

Zamboanga Agri-Farm Products Inc. is an egg producer serving wholesale buyers in Zamboanga City, Jolo Island, and Tawi-Tawi. This capstone focuses on improving how production data is captured, reviewed, and used by management by moving away from delayed paper reporting toward same-day operational visibility.

2 sitesCurrent reporting flow spans multiple locations with manual consolidation.
T+1Production visibility arrives late under the current paper-based process.
24 housesActivation target for rollout and adoption planning.

The homepage simulation is a visual shorthand for that transformation: each egg pass represents a production event becoming a record, moving the operation from lagging updates to faster review, stronger data integrity, and tighter management control.
