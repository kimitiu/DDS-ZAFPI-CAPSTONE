About

ZAFPI’s reporting modernization initiative.

Zamboanga Agri-Farm Products Inc. is an egg producer serving wholesale buyers in Zamboanga City, Jolo Island, and Tawi-Tawi. This capstone translates an operations improvement effort into a portfolio-ready story: replacing delayed, paper-heavy production reporting with a workflow designed for same-day visibility, cleaner data, stronger review, and tighter management control.

2 sitesProduction reporting spans more than one location, which increases coordination difficulty.
T+1Current production visibility arrives after delay rather than during the same operating day.
24 housesActivation target used to frame scale, rollout scope, and adoption planning.
95%+Daily encoding compliance target within 24 hours during rollout and go-live.
Context

This project is not just about replacing paper with a screen. It is about changing the rhythm of operational decision-making. When production records arrive late and require reconciliation before they are trusted, management loses the ability to review the day while it is still actionable.

Current state
Paper-based records are generated and later consolidated, which makes review reactive.
Target state
Production events are encoded earlier, checked faster, and made visible for same-day operational follow-up.
Expected effect
Better data integrity, more disciplined daily review, and stronger control over utilization and rollout progress.
