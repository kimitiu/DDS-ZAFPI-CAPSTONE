Overview

A capstone built around ZAFPI’s operating reality.

Project Farmbrite is a capstone initiative for Zamboanga Agri-Farm Products Inc., an egg producer based in Zamboanga City. The project focuses on improving production reporting, operational visibility, and control across a two-site operation that serves wholesale markets in Zamboanga City, Jolo Island, and Tawi-Tawi.

The work matters because ZAFPI’s reporting discipline directly affects how quickly management can respond to production issues, monitor house-level performance, and make better planning decisions.

Why this project matters

Project Farmbrite is not framed as a generic system installation. It is a management-control initiative designed to replace delayed paper reporting with same-day visibility, stronger data integrity, and more proactive farm oversight.

Client ZAFPI
Zamboanga Agri-Farm Products Inc. is an egg producer and distributor operating from Zamboanga City.
Operating structure 2 sites
Operations are split between the farm or production site and the sorting and distribution center.

Operational context

What ZAFPI produces

ZAFPI specializes in egg production and distribution, serving wholesale buyers across multiple markets with a sustained production footprint.

Where it operates

The business is based in Zamboanga City and serves Zamboanga City, Jolo Island, and Tawi-Tawi through its farm and distribution operations.

Why the company matters

Its scale, multi-site structure, and reporting discipline make it a strong case for operational redesign rather than a small isolated systems fix.

Project framing
Problem focusDelayed manual production reporting, fragmented data, and weak operational visibility.
Business goalSupport same-day visibility, stronger reporting discipline, and more proactive farm management.
Control focusStandardize reporting, improve integrity checks, and enable more confident review at the management level.
Project directionUse structured digital capture, dashboards, validations, and auditability to improve operational control.
Why the capstone matters

Current state

Before Project Farmbrite, management visibility arrived late and required significant manual effort before data could be trusted.

  • Paper-based production recording across two sites
  • T+1 reporting and delayed operational review
  • 43% weekly reconciliation burden
  • Weak house-level visibility for feed and mortality

Target state

The capstone moves the conversation toward structured reporting discipline and better management control.

  • Same-day digital production capture
  • Cleaner reporting discipline and data integrity checks
  • Less than 15% reconciliation target
  • Dashboards and reporting for operational review
