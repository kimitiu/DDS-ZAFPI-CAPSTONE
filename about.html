About

ZAFPI’s reporting modernization initiative.

Project Farmbrite is a data-driven operational transformation initiative for Zamboanga Agri-Farm Products Inc. It reframes production reporting as an operating-control problem, then redesigns the process around structured digital capture, same-day visibility, and stronger management review using Farmbrite.

Egg production + wholesaleZAFPI operates as an egg producer serving wholesale buyers through a farm site and a sorting and distribution center.
3 markets servedZamboanga City, Jolo Island, and Tawi-Tawi define the operating geography reflected throughout the project.
24-house footprintThe project covers 19 Layer Houses, 2 Brooder Houses, and 3 Grower Houses.
57% utilization baselineThe capstone frames underutilization as a visibility and management-control problem, not just a production issue.
Objectives
Operational objectiveDigitize production reporting, standardize workflows, centralize data, and reduce manual error.
Data objectiveCapture structured daily data for egg output, rejects, mortality, flock age and headcount, and feed issuance and consumption.
System objectiveUse dashboards, validation rules, audit trails, role-based access, and automated reports to support control.
Positioning
BeforeManual, delayed, fragmented
AfterDigital, same-day, auditable

Project Farmbrite is positioned as an operational management system, not just a software installation. The website uses the simulation to make that shift legible, while the inner pages translate the project into a proper capstone portfolio record.
